The Mathematics of Relevance

We convert both your search query and our documents into numeric vectors. The similarity is calculated using the Cosine formula, measuring the orientation rather than magnitude.

The Formula

sim(A,B) = (A · B) / (||A|| ||B||)

The dot product of two vectors divided by the product of their magnitudes.
